About Me


I am a dedicated Game Developer and Designer with a strong passion for creating impactful, immersive game experiences. Currently pursuing a MS in Game Science and Design, I focus on blending creativity with technical skills to design games that engage and challenge players on multiple levels. My experience spans core gameplay, game mechanics, physics, cloud management, and user interactions, and I've developed diverse projects in both Unity and Unreal, specializing in elements that bring depth and innovation to each game.

Beyond game development, I am deeply interested in the possibilities at the intersection of artificial intelligence and games. With experience in machine learning and AI tools in Python, I envision a future where these technologies play a significant role in creating games that are not only more intelligent but capable of meaningful, positive impact. My goal is to explore and push the boundaries of AI within game development, aiming to craft experiences that go beyond entertainment to offer something truly unique and beneficial for players and the industry.

I am also committed to collaboration and have had the opportunity to lead technical teams and organize major events, helping me develop strong project management and team-building skills. Looking ahead, I am excited to contribute to the evolution of game design, combining AI and innovative gameplay to create experiences that can redefine what games can accomplish.
